Chicken Marsala

Two chicken breasts
A pint of Mushrooms (your choice)
Butter
Marsala Wine

Pasta
Sauce

Butterfly the chicken breasts.  Lightly salt and pepper the thinly sliced chicken breats.

Put a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and heat.  

Once pan is hot begin cooking the chicken breats.  Once chicken is almost cooked add mushrooms.

After the mushrooms are lightly sauted add butter and wine. 

Serve hot.

You may want to include pasta and sauce.

Garlic bread is good too.
